## Every other tool collects. WandKit resolves.

This is the difference.

Traditional tools capture what happened. WandKit understands why it happened — and
helps you fix it. The AI layer connects signals across every module: feedback, behavior,
language, navigation. It closes the loop.

**Example:**
A user hits a friction point during onboarding. WandKit detects the drop-off, surfaces
a targeted survey, interprets the response, and recommends a copy change — which you
push server-side without shipping an update.

No ticket. No release. No waiting.

## Ridiculously simple to integrate

```swift
WandKit.configure(apiKey: "...")
WandKit.identify("user_123")
WandKit.event("onboarding_finished")
```

That's the entire integration. The platform handles the rest — when to show a survey,
what to ask, who to ask, and what to do with the answer.

- Native SwiftUI overlay — not a WebView
- iOS 15+ and macOS 10.15+
- Swift Package Manager
- Fully themeable
